How APDA Tournaments Work (60-second primer)

  • Schedule: Most run Fri–Sat (occasional Sun). Expect an opening assembly, 5+ prelim rounds, then elims.
  • Break: Teams with strong prelim records “break” to elimination rounds; exact cut depends on field size.
  • Speaks & ranks: Judges award speaker points and ranks each round; totals determine breaks and awards.
  • Judging: Experienced judges “flow” rounds, weigh comparative strength, assign speaks, and call winners.
  • Pairing: A tab system pairs teams each round based on records and constraints.

Logistics & Costs

Costs

$0 to compete. PDU covers travel, registration, and lodging.

Travel & ID

We travel by train/bus/van depending on distance. Bring a valid photo ID. Exact meet-up times are in each TID.

Meals

Hosts usually provide lunch on Day 1 and breakfast/lunch on Day 2. Pack snacks and a water bottle.

Absence letters

Need a letter for professors? Contact the Operations Director—we’ll coordinate.

Day-of updates

Urgent changes appear on the Calendar and via email/Discord. Check both during travel weekends.

Partners & Roles

Partners are requested or assigned in person on a per-tournament basis.

  • Have a preferred partner? Tell us—we’ll try to honor it based on availability.
  • New or flexible? We’ll pair you with someone compatible.
  • Not a fit? Partnerships can change between events—normal.

Equity & Safety

Follow host equity policies and PDU standards: debate ideas, not identities; use Content Warnings (CWs) and honor opt-outs. Report concerns to the Equity Team or a PDU officer immediately.
