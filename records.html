Internal Records Archive

Semester-by-semester materials, outcomes, and governance notes for internal use.

Use this archive to review past tournament activity, membership operations, budgeting, and strategic notes. Materials are structured for continuity and handoffs between leadership teams.

Restricted circulation Updated each semester close Board + committee access

Archive Overview

The internal archive consolidates all institutional materials required for continuity across academic years. Each record bundle includes leadership notes, competitive results, finance summaries, and operational documentation approved by the Executive Board.

Confidential materials include travel receipts, eligibility notes, and meeting attendance logs. Share internally only with current officers, tournament captains, and approved advisors.

Record Categories

Each semester archive maintains a consistent structure for quick handoffs.

Governance

Constitution updates, election results, officer transition checklists, and policy amendments.

Competitive Results

Tournament attendance, performance summaries, judge sign-ups, and case bank highlights.

Finance

Budget allocations, funding requests, receipts, reimbursement logs, and end-of-term close.

Membership

Attendance trends, roster updates, leadership pipeline notes, and engagement metrics.

Access & Requests

Who can access

  • Executive Board members and committee chairs.
  • Faculty advisors or approved administrators.
  • Captains with active tournament logistics responsibilities.

Request workflow

  • Submit request to Operations Director with semester + category.
  • Confirm need-to-know and intended use.
  • Receive access within 48 hours during the academic term.
Contact Operations →

Retention Standards

Records are stored to maintain continuity while respecting privacy and compliance requirements.

  • Retention: Minimum of 4 academic years for official financial and governance records.
  • Privacy: Redact personal data before sharing outside the board.
  • Updates: Archive is finalized within 30 days of each semester close.
