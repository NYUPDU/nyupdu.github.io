About the Executive Board

PDU’s Executive Board (“E-Board”) works as one team to plan meetings, coach novices, coordinate travel, uphold equity, and represent NYU on the APDA circuit. Officers meet regularly to align training goals, publish TIDs, and ensure a smooth, supportive season for all members.
