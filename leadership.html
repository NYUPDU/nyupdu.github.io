About the Executive Board

The PDU Executive Board sets vision and keeps the club running: practice curriculum, travel logistics, tournament entries, team culture and equity, communications, and funding. We meet regularly to coordinate training, select tournaments, and support members at every level. Questions or ideas? Reach out — we’re here to help.
