Weighing Coach

Compose crisp, comparative weighing lines. Pick an axis, then fill the template.

Pick axes

Examples

  • “Even if they get uptake, our policy reaches more people sooner — higher magnitude, faster timeframe.”
  • “Their harm is low-probability and reversible; ours is likely and hard to reverse.”

Tip: speak the weighing line twice—when introducing an argument and again in rebuttal.

POO Assistant

Quick phrasing + simple checker for “new in rebuttal”.

Sample phrasing

Is it POO-able?

Rebuttals are LOR & PMR. New material there → valid POO; constructives (PMC/LOC/MG/MO) → not a POO for novelty.
